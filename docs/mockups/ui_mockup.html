Chapter 01 · Verbs
て-form Conjugation

The て-form is one of the most important verb forms in Japanese. It is used to connect actions, make requests, and build more complex sentences.

Group 1 verbs (godan)

The ending changes based on the final syllable:

書く → 書いて
kaku → kaite (to write)
飲む → 飲んで
nomu → nonde (to drink)

Group 2 verbs (ichidan)

食べる → 食べて
taberu → tabete (to eat)
