Chapter 01 · Verbs
て-form Conjugation
N5
verbs
conjugation
difficulty ●●●

The て-form is one of the most versatile verb forms in Japanese. It connects actions, forms requests, and builds compound sentences.

Group 1 — Godan verbs

The ending changes based on the final syllable of the verb stem:

書く → 書いて
kaku → kaite  ·  to write
飲む → 飲んで
nomu → nonde  ·  to drink
話す → 話して
hanasu → hanashite  ·  to speak

Group 2 — Ichidan verbs

Simply drop る and add て:

食べる → 食べて
taberu → tabete  ·  to eat
