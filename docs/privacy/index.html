Privacy Policy

Manabi Do — Last updated: June 2026

Manabi Do is a Japanese language learning app. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data stored on your device

All learning progress is stored locally on your device:

  • SRS card state (review dates, intervals, ease factors)
  • Known / unknown flags for kana, kanji, and vocabulary
  • App settings (theme, language, daily card limit)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sign in to enable cloud sync (see below). Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data.

Account and cloud sync (optional)

You may use the app as a guest with no account required. If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, your SRS progress is synced to your account so it is not lost if you switch devices or reinstall the app.

Sign-in is handled by Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple respectively. Manabi Do only stores your user ID and learning progress data — not your name, email address, or any other profile information.

Text-to-speech

Pronunciation playback uses the speech synthesis engine built into your device's operating system. No audio data is sent to external servers.

Analytics and advertising

Manabi Do does not use any advertising or third-party analytics. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Children

Manabi Do does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app does not require an account and collects no personal information in guest mode.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
